Featured · Patent-Filed

Battery Passport & on-device fault intelligence

A patent-filed system that fuses deep learning, swarm optimization and electrochemistry to estimate the state of an EV battery — its charge, health and degradation — directly on the BMS, in real time, without ever needing labeled data. Built at Suavy Technologies for use in battery swapping stations.

Estimated parameters · Equivalent Circuit Model
R0R1R2C1C2ηM0M
  • Hybrid pipeline. ANN delivers fast initial parameter prediction; PSO refines them against measured terminal-voltage residuals — combining speed with precision.
  • Physics-informed core. The model isn't a black box. It is anchored to a 2-RC Thévenin equivalent circuit, ensuring estimates remain physically plausible across temperature and aging.
  • No labels needed. Self-supervised pipeline learns directly from voltage / current / temperature streams — solving the dataset bottleneck that blocks most BMS ML work.
  • Lifecycle & warranty. Outputs are cryptographically sealed in a tamper-resistant Battery Passport — enabling fleet-level safety, second-life tracking and warranty enforcement.
  • Real-time fault signal. Drift in estimated parameters acts as an early-warning signal for cell degradation, internal short, and capacity fade.
